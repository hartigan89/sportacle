New Player FAQ

What is Sportacle?

Sportacle is a web-based game that evaluates your ability to pick the outcome of sporting events, while taking into consideration the odds of each event.

How do I play?

Create an account and then proceed to login. Once logged in, access the Gamelist page which will list the upcoming sporting events. Select your expected results of the events. Sportacle will do the rest of the work and place you into a rank based on your skill.

What is my goal while playing?

Your goal is to be the most profitable & consistent sports picker. The odds are taken into consideation and are displayed on the Gamelist page.
Follower System FAQ

What is the follower system?

As a user you have the ability to follow other user's. By doing so, you gain the ability to view their selected upcoming picks. However, if the user is the rank of Oracle, it will cost you to follow them.
Ranking System FAQ

How does the ranking system work?

Sportacle matches your expected picks with the actual sport events results. Your picks are then run through Sportacles ranking system algorithim. The algorithim's criteria includes how profitable a player is, and how consistent they are.

What are the ranks?

Each rank has three tiers. For example Amateur III is a higher rank than Amateur I. The ranks can be found below:
  • Rookie I, II, III
  • Amateur I, II, III
  • Pro I, II, III
  • Oracle I, II, III

How do I earn money from my picks?

If your rank reaches the level of "Oracle" then it will cost other users to follow you, and this is when you will begin to receive money.

How do I find an Oracle to follow?

The Leaderboard is the best place to find the best Oracles. However, the better oracles are more costly to follow as they are proven to be more profitable.

How do I receive the money as a Oracle?

When a user follows you, the money will be deposited into a PayPal account of your choosing.
